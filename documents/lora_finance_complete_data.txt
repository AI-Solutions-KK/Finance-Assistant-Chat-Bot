3. PERSONAL LOAN SERVICES
================================================================================

3.1 OVERVIEW
Unsecured personal loans for salaried and self-employed individuals for any 
genuine personal need - medical emergencies, education, travel, weddings, 
home renovation, debt consolidation, etc.

3.2 KEY FEATURES
- Loan Amount: ₹25,000 to ₹25 Lakhs
- Interest Rate: 10.5% to 18% per annum
- Tenure: 6 months to 60 months (5 years)
- Processing Fee: 2% of loan amount
- Processing Time: 24-48 hours

3.3 PERSONAL LOAN INTEREST RATES
For Salaried Employees:
- Excellent credit score (750+): 10.5% - 12% p.a.
- Good credit score (700-749): 12% - 14% p.a.
- Fair credit score (650-699): 14% - 16% p.a.
- Below 650: 16% - 18% p.a.

For Self-Employed:
- Annual income >₹10 Lakhs: 12% - 14% p.a.
- Annual income ₹5-10 Lakhs: 14% - 16% p.a.
- Annual income <₹5 Lakhs: 16% - 18% p.a.

3.4 ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
For Salaried Individuals:
- Age: 21 to 60 years
- Minimum monthly income: ₹25,000
- Work experience: Minimum 1 year (6 months in current company)
- Credit score: Minimum 650

For Self-Employed:
- Age: 25 to 65 years
- Business vintage: Minimum 3 years
- Annual income: Minimum ₹3 Lakhs
- Credit score: Minimum 650
- ITR for last 2 years mandatory

3.5 DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR PERSONAL LOAN
For Salaried:
- PAN Card (mandatory)
- Aadhaar Card / Passport / Voter ID
- Last 6 months salary slips
- Last 6 months bank statements
- Employment letter / ID card
- Form 16 / ITR (for loans above ₹5 Lakhs)

For Self-Employed:
- PAN Card (mandatory)
- Aadhaar Card / Passport / Voter ID
- Business proof (GST certificate / Trade license / Shop Act)
- Last 12 months bank statements
- ITR and computation for last 2 years
- Profit & Loss statement and Balance Sheet

3.6 LOAN AMOUNT CALCULATION
Maximum loan amount = Monthly income × 60 (for salaried)
Maximum loan amount = Annual income × 5 (for self-employed)
Subject to credit assessment and repayment capacity

3.7 CHARGES & FEES
- Processing Fee: 2% (negotiable based on profile)
- Late payment charges: ₹500 per installment
- Cheque bounce charges: ₹750 per instance
- Loan cancellation: ₹2,000 (if cancelled after disbursal)
- Foreclosure charges: 4% of outstanding principal (after 6 months)
- Part-payment: 2% of part-payment amount

11. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)
================================================================================

11.1 GOLD LOAN FAQS

Q: How quickly can I get a gold loan?
A: Our gold loans are processed in just 30 minutes. Visit any branch with your 
gold and documents, and walk out with cash.

Q: Is my gold safe with Lora Finance?
A: Yes, absolutely. Your gold is stored in bank-grade lockers with 24/7 CCTV 
surveillance and comprehensive insurance coverage.

Q: What if gold prices fall during my loan tenure?
A: We provide a buffer margin. However, if gold value falls below loan amount, 
we may request additional collateral or partial repayment.

Q: Can I withdraw my gold before full repayment?
A: Yes, we offer partial release facility. You can pay down a portion of your 
loan and take back proportionate gold.


11.2 PERSONAL LOAN FAQS

Q: What is the minimum credit score required?
A: We prefer a credit score of 650 or above. However, we evaluate applications 
holistically and may consider lower scores with strong income proof.

Q: How is my loan eligibility calculated?
A: Eligibility depends on your monthly income, existing obligations, credit 
score, employment stability, and repayment capacity.

Q: Can I increase my loan amount later?
A: Yes, after 6 months of regular repayment, you can apply for a top-up loan 
at competitive rates.

Q: What happens if I miss an EMI payment?
A: You'll be charged a late payment fee of ₹500. Multiple missed payments will 
affect your credit score and may lead to recovery proceedings.


11.3 GENERAL FAQS

Q: Do you offer pre-approved loans?
A: Yes, existing customers with good repayment track records are offered 
pre-approved loans with instant disbursal.

Q: Can I apply online?
A: Yes, visit www.lorafinance.com and complete the application form. Our team 
will contact you within 24 hours.

Q: Is Aadhaar mandatory for loan application?
A: Aadhaar is not mandatory but preferred. You can submit any other government-
issued photo ID along with address proof.

Q: How do I check my loan status?
A: Login to our customer portal at www.lorafinance.com/login or call our 
customer care for real-time status updates.
